# Channel Metrics Catalog

Reference for marketing metrics by channel. Each metric includes its definition, formula, typical benchmark ranges, and what directional movements signal.

## Email Marketing

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Delivery rate | Delivered / Sent | 95-99% | Healthy list, good reputation | List decay, blacklisting risk |
| Open rate | Unique opens / Delivered | 15-30% | Subject lines resonate | Fatigue, poor timing, deliverability issues |
| Click-through rate (CTR) | Unique clicks / Delivered | 2-5% | Content and CTAs are compelling | Irrelevant content or poor CTA placement |
| Click-to-open rate (CTOR) | Unique clicks / Unique opens | 10-20% | Email body is effective (for openers) | Content doesn't match subject line promise |
| Unsubscribe rate | Unsubscribes / Delivered | <0.5% | Normal churn | Over-mailing, poor targeting, irrelevant content |
| Bounce rate | Bounces / Sent | <2% | Clean list | Stale data, bad acquisition sources |
| Conversion rate | Conversions / Delivered | 1-5% | Offer resonates with audience | Friction in conversion path or weak offer |
| Revenue per email | Revenue / Sent | Varies by industry | Strong monetization | Audience mismatch or offer fatigue |
| List growth rate | (New - Unsubscribes) / Total list | 2-5% monthly | Healthy acquisition | Acquisition not keeping up with churn |

## Social Media

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Impressions | Times content displayed | Varies by platform | Growing distribution | Algorithm deprioritization or lower posting |
| Reach | Unique users who saw content | Varies by platform | Broader audience | Shrinking addressable audience |
| Engagement rate | (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach | 1-5% | Content resonates | Content fatigue or misaligned topics |
| Click-through rate | Link clicks / Impressions | 0.5-2% | Effective traffic driving | Weak CTAs or irrelevant audience |
| Follower growth rate | Net new followers / Total followers | 1-3% monthly | Brand momentum | Brand perception issues or content quality |
| Share/Repost rate | Shares / Reach | 0.1-1% | High-value, shareable content | Content isn't compelling enough to share |
| Video view rate | Views / Impressions | 15-30% | Strong hook/thumbnail | Weak creative or wrong audience |
| Video completion rate | Completed views / Total views | 15-40% | Right length and content | Too long, loses interest, poor pacing |
| Share of voice | Your mentions / Category mentions | Varies | Growing mindshare | Competitors gaining ground |

## Paid Advertising (Search & Social)

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Impressions | Times ad shown | Varies | Budget utilization, broad targeting | Budget limits, narrow targeting |
| Click-through rate (CTR) | Clicks / Impressions | 2-5% (search), 0.5-1.5% (display) | Relevant creative and targeting | Ad fatigue, poor targeting |
| Cost per click (CPC) | Spend / Clicks | $1-5 (varies by industry) | Increasing competition or lower quality | Better optimization or less competition |
| Cost per mille (CPM) | (Spend / Impressions) * 1000 | $5-30 | Competitive auction, premium placement | Efficient awareness buying |
| Conversion rate | Conversions / Clicks | 2-10% | Strong landing page and offer | Friction in conversion path |
| Cost per acquisition (CPA) | Spend / Conversions | Varies by industry | Inefficiency growing | Better optimization |
| Return on ad spend (ROAS) | Revenue / Spend | 3-5x | Profitable campaigns | Diminishing returns or scaling issues |
| Quality Score (search) | Google's relevance rating | 7-10 | Good ad-keyword-landing alignment | Misalignment, review ad copy and landing page |
| Frequency | Impressions / Unique users | <3-4 per week | Repeated exposure for awareness | Ad fatigue risk (creative rotation needed) |

## SEO / Organic Search

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Organic sessions | Visits from organic search | Varies | SEO strategy working | Algorithm changes, ranking losses |
| Keyword rankings | Position for target keywords | Page 1 (top 10) | Content and authority growing | Competitors outranking, content decay |
| Organic CTR | Clicks / Impressions in SERP | 2-10% (position dependent) | Compelling title and meta | Poor SERP presentation |
| Pages indexed | Pages in search index | Varies | Good crawlability | Indexing issues, thin content |
| Domain authority | Third-party authority score | 30-70 (varies) | Growing backlink profile | Lost links, competitor growth |
| Backlinks | External sites linking to you | Growing over time | Content earning citations | Link rot, content becoming outdated |
| Organic conversion rate | Organic conversions / Organic sessions | 1-5% | Good intent matching | Content attracts wrong audience |

## Content Marketing

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Pageviews | Total views of content | Varies | Distribution working | Declining promotion or SEO |
| Unique visitors | Distinct users viewing content | Varies | Growing audience | Shrinking reach |
| Average time on page | Total time / Pageviews | 2-5 minutes | Engaging, in-depth content | Content too thin or irrelevant |
| Bounce rate | Single-page sessions / Total sessions | 40-60% | — | Poor UX, mismatched expectations, or content-audience misfit |
| Scroll depth | % of page scrolled | 50-70% | Users consuming full content | Losing interest, too long, poor formatting |
| Social shares | Times content shared on social | Varies | Resonant, shareable content | Not compelling enough to share |
| Lead generation | Leads attributed to content | Varies | Effective CTAs and offers | Poor conversion path or weak offer |
| Content ROI | Revenue attributed / Production cost | >3x over 12 months | Worthwhile investment | Reconsider topic, format, or distribution |

## Pipeline and Revenue (Overall Marketing)

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark | Up means | Down means |
|--------|---------|-----------|----------|------------|
| Marketing qualified leads (MQLs) | Leads meeting marketing criteria | Varies | Top-of-funnel is healthy | Acquisition channels underperforming |
| Sales qualified leads (SQLs) | MQLs accepted by sales | Varies | Good lead quality | Lead quality issues or misaligned criteria |
| MQL to SQL rate | SQLs / MQLs | 20-40% | Marketing-sales alignment | Criteria mismatch or poor lead nurture |
| Pipeline generated | Dollar value of opportunities | Varies | Marketing driving revenue | Campaign or targeting issues |
| Pipeline velocity | Pipeline / Average deal cycle | Varies | Deals moving faster | Longer sales cycles, friction |
| Customer acquisition cost (CAC) | (Marketing + Sales cost) / New customers | Varies | Efficiency improving | Scaling costs outpacing growth |
| CAC payback period | CAC / Monthly revenue per customer | <12 months | Healthy unit economics | Unsustainable if >18 months |
| LTV:CAC ratio | Customer lifetime value / CAC | 3:1 or better | Profitable customer base | Acquisition costs too high or retention issues |
